Ribs are a cut of meat from the ribcage of a domestic pig, beef, or sheep. They are a popular food in many cuisines around the world. The most common types of ribs are pork ribs, which are taken from the pig's ribcage. Pork ribs can be cooked in a variety of ways, including grilling, baking, and smoking. They are often seasoned with a dry rub or marinade to add flavor. Pork ribs are typically served with a barbecue sauce or other condiments.
