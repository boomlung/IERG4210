Lanna noodles are a type of noodle dish that is popular in the northern region of Thailand. The dish consists of rice noodles that are stir-fried with a variety of ingredients, including meat, vegetables, and spices. Lanna noodles are known for their bold flavors and unique combination of ingredients, making them a favorite among locals and tourists alike.
